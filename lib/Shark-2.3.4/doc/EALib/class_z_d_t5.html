Detailed Description

ConstraintHandler for the feasible region being a cuboid.

The feasible region is an n-dimensional cuboid with the side length in the first dimension differing from the side lengths in the other dimensions.

ConstraintHandler suitable for LZ07_F6

The feasible region is an n-dimensional cuboid with the side length in the first and second dimension differing from the side lengths in the other dimensions.
